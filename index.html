What is the Science of Creative Intelligence?

This application explores the 16 principles of the Science of Creative Intelligence (SCI). SCI provides a complete vision of the field that is fundamental to our existence: the Unified Field of pure consciousness. This field is home to the laws that govern all natural phenomena and all fields of knowledge.

The aim of this knowledge is to complete education by adding the "subjective" aspect (knowledge of the knower) to the "objective" aspect (knowledge of the world). By understanding these principles, we can develop our full potential and live a life of fulfillment and harmony.
